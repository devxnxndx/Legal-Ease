Legal Ease AI

Legal Ease AI is an AI-powered legal assistant designed to make legal guidance more accessible, understandable, and multilingual for users across India.
It processes legal documents (PDFs), provides simplified legal insights, and delivers responses in 22 Indian languages with text-to-speech (TTS) support.
